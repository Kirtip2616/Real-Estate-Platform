Locality Guide
for Sewri, Mumbai

Share
Sewri, located in southern part of Mumbai is predominantly an industrial area with beautiful sea ports and government warehouses. Sewri contains some low-rise residential homes and in the recent past, it has seen some great residential and township developments like Peninsula Celestia Spaces, Monarch Garden, Peninsula Ashok Garden, Dosti Flamingo etc. which look very promising owing to the locality’s proximity employment hubs and public transportation. Neighbouring locations of Sewri are Parel, Mazgaon, Wadala etc. Harbour Line, Monrail and Eastern Freeway are some of the best connectivity routes which connect Sewri to Navi Mumbai, Central Mumbai etc. Currey Road and Lower Parel railway station are some of the nearest railway stations to Sewri. Chhatrapati International Airport is situated 15 km away from the location and can be easily reached via Sahara Airport Road. Some of the great educational institutions such as schools, colleges and universities situated in Sewri are JBCN International School, Fatima High School, SIES High School, Mumbai Veterinary College etc. Great recreational spaces like malls ad sports centres in ad around Sewri are Palladium Mall, Atria Mall, GSB Sabha Sports Club, Dadar Parsee Colony Gymkhana etc. Read More
Currey Road railway station offers a great connectiity to the commuters
Mumbai International Airport is 15 km away from Sewri and can be eaily reached via road
Lower Parel railway station has a proper transportation system to connect different areas of the city
Various new residential and township projects are set up in Sewri
What is good here
  • Currey Road railway station connects Sewri to all the neighbouring places of the locality effortlesslyread more

  • Many kids friendly parks are available in Sewri such as Bhoiwada Park
What can be better
  • Some buses are not on time and this causes issues to the daily commuters
